About Us

A Honolulu studio built on craft, community, and the love of making things well.

Our Story

Thread Works Studios opened its doors in 2023, right on the edge of Honolulu's Chinatown — a neighborhood with deep roots in craft and commerce. The studio is new, but the hands behind it aren't. Our team brings over 40 years of combined professional sewing experience to every project that comes through the door.

We work on everything — production runs for local brands, alterations for neighbors, custom pieces for people with a vision. No project is too big or too small. We just love the work.

If you have something you want made, repaired, or brought back to life — come find us. We'd love to meet you.
