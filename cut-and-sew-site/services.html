Full-Scale Sewing Production

We are a full-service sewing production studio built for volume. Whether you're a local clothing brand, a business needing uniforms, or a designer ready to scale — we handle complete sewing runs with consistent quality from the first piece to the last. In-house bulk ironing and finishing included.

  • Bags, totes & accessories

  • Uniforms & workwear

Alterations & Repairs

A great fit changes everything. We do expert alterations and repairs on garments of all kinds — quickly, carefully, and often while you wait. Many jobs are done same-day or with a very short turnaround.

  • Hemming — cuffed, plain, blind, rolled, original seam & chainstitch
  • Dresses — hemming, taking in, straps, lining & structure adjustments
  • Taking in & letting out seams
  • Waist & seat adjustments
  • Sleeve shortening & reshaping
  • Zippers, buttons & closures
  • General mending & repairs
  • While-you-wait service available
